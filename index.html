    The Wardrobe

    Project:

    The Wardrobe:

    Branding, Logotype, Webpage



    Branding for The Wardrobe. A library for clothes. It's currently a permanent part in Fashion Gallery in Textile Fashion Centre, Borås, together with the Textile Museum of Sweden.

    The wardrobe was a project to raise awareness to the current fashion industry. It focused on preserving vintage clothes. The result was a library of clothes featured in several magazines and mentioned in nation wide press.

    The project involved branding; creating of logo, flyers, tags, interior design, as well as design and develop their website.

    Linn Larsson, ICA Banken

    Honey the Food is Ready!


    Art Directed and designed Book Cover for creator Linn Larsson.

    Linn Larsson is a talented Swedish food blogger. Since 2010 Linn has been blogging for the largest food company in Sweden, ICA. Previously she released two books, for her third book she asked me to produce a cover. I put together some proposals including photos and details from the book as well as added some more graphics to the design.

    The book has been featured in Swedish newspapers and magazines, sold in stores such as DesignTorget and Åhlens and sold over 20 000 copies.

    Makerclub

    Project:

    Makerclub

    Art Direction and Design

    Start-up Makerclub teaches kids how to code through robotics and using 3D printing. For the first three months I helped Founder Simon and Declan to set up the branding for the organisation.

    For Makerclub I created Branding for website, banners, businesscards, flyers as well as event material for their Oggbot project during Brighton Digital Festival 2014. The Oggbot event was attended by over 100 families and introduced kids(and their parents) to try out robotics and code.

    Today Makerclub are successfully running a 3D printing lab in Brighton, UK, as well as teaching kids and adults how to code.
